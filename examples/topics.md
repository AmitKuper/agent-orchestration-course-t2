# Debate Topics

Topics to use for generating example debate conversations.

---

## Technology & AI

- AI will replace most human jobs within 20 years
- Social media does more harm than good to society
- Open-source AI models should be freely available without restrictions
- Governments should regulate AI development

## Geopolitics & Security

- Will a military strike on Iran eliminate the nuclear threat, or will diplomacy achieve it better?

## Sports

- Who is the better football player: Messi or Ronaldo?

## Ethics & Society

- Universal Basic Income should be implemented globally
- The death penalty should be abolished worldwide
- Zoos are unethical and should be phased out
- Eating meat is ethically justifiable

## Science & Environment

- Nuclear energy is the best solution to climate change
- Space exploration funding should be prioritized over solving Earth's problems
- Genetic engineering of humans should be permitted

## Education & Culture

- Homework should be abolished in schools
- College education should be free for everyone
- Video games have a net positive effect on young people
